What is XenCL?

XenCL aims to provide an easy interface for operating with XenForo online communities.

Other Guff

XenCL is a sub-project of ED-Bot ("Colleen"), licensed under the Artistic License 2.0 and Â©2013 TymoonNET/NexT, Nicolas Hafner.
This library can be obtained via git on git://git.tymoon.eu/xencl.git. For questions, patches or suggestions, please contact me via email.
